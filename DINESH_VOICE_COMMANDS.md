1. **Speak Clearly**: Enunciate words for better recognition
2. **Be Specific**: "Book a service" is better than just "book"
3. **Natural Language**: Speak naturally, don't use robot commands
4. **Wait for Response**: Let Dinesh finish before next command

- No difference in functionality
